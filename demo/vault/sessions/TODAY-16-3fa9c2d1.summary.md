# {{TODAY-16}} — naming the jury

First conversation. Nora arrived the morning after a tense evening with Devon —
a money-adjacent comment, then a long silent table-clearing that the inner voice
spent all night prosecuting. She named the voice herself: **the jury**. It has
been around since art school; freelancing gave it a courtroom that never closes.

What mattered: saying out loud that Devon has never actually delivered a verdict —
the sentence is drafted entirely in-house. She laughed at that, and the laugh
loosened something.

What helps, in her own words: naming the feeling plainly, the river walk before
the phone, her sister Tess.

Agreed to work toward: noticing the jury early instead of arguing with it late,
and finding her way to the money conversation with Devon on her own terms.

Follow-ups: a portfolio deadline next week — the jury's favourite season.
